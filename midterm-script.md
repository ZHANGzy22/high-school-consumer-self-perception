I am building an interactive shopping platform simulation for high school students. The project focuses on one question: when students describe themselves as consumers, do their choices in a realistic shopping situation tell the same story?

## Slide 2: Research Question & Hypothesis

English:

The simulation first records what users do, and then shows them a comparison between those choices and their self-perception.

My research question is: **How does an interactive shopping platform simulation affect high school students' recognition of gaps between their consumer self-perception and their observed choices?**

My hypothesis is: **I hypothesize that after using the interactive shopping platform simulation, high school students will become more aware of the gap between what they think they will do and what they actually choose.**

The core logic is simple: self-perception leads to observed choice, and the comparison makes a possible gap visible.

中文翻译：

这个模拟器先记录用户实际做了什么，再把这些选择和他们的自我认知进行比较。

我的研究问题是：**一个互动式购物平台模拟器如何影响高中生对消费自我认知和可观察选择之间差距的认识？**

我的假设是：**我假设，使用互动式购物平台模拟器之后，高中生会更意识到自己认为会做什么和实际选择什么之间的差距。**

核心逻辑很简单：自我认知带来可观察选择，而这两者的比较让可能存在的差距变得可见。

## Slide 3: Background

English:

Consumer choices are now part of students' social and digital lives. A shopping environment can change what they notice and what feels reasonable before they decide.

Many students see themselves as careful or budget-conscious consumers. When that self-image and a later choice differ, the difference is meaningful: it shows personal judgment operating inside a socially designed environment.

中文翻译：

消费选择已经是学生社会生活和数字生活的一部分。购物环境会影响他们注意到什么，也会影响什么在决定前看起来合理。

很多学生会把自己看作谨慎或有预算意识的消费者。当这种自我形象和之后的选择不同，这个差异就有意义：它展示了个人判断如何在社会设计出的环境中运作。

## Slide 4: Literature Review

English:

The literature gives me three linked ideas.

First, advertising literacy can help students recognize persuasion, yet this knowledge may not guide action at the moment of choice. Second, self-report answers can describe an ideal self-image, so they cannot stand alone as evidence of behavior. Third, consumer judgment has several dimensions, and shopping-platform design can influence attention and decision-making.

Together, these findings show why asking students about their habits is not enough. Consumer judgment needs both self-perception and observed behavior.

The main gap is clear: previous work rarely compares these two sides for high school students in one controlled, realistic shopping experience. My project does this through a shopping simulation and interaction records.

中文翻译：

这些文献给了我三个相互关联的想法。

第一，广告素养可以帮助学生识别说服性信息，但这种知识在选择当下未必会指导行动。第二，自我报告可能描述理想化的自我形象，所以它不能单独作为行为证据。第三，消费判断有多个维度，购物平台的设计也会影响注意力和决策。

这些发现说明，只问学生如何看待自己的消费习惯是不够的。消费判断需要同时看自我认知和可观察行为。

主要缺口很清楚：已有研究很少在同一个受控且真实感较强的购物体验中，比较高中生这两方面的表现。我的项目用购物模拟器和互动记录完成这种比较。

## Slide 5: Research Design / Method

English:

This literature gap directly shapes the design of my artifact.

Users first answer short questions about budget range, product interests, and shopping habits. They then complete a neutral self-perception scale with five response options.

Next, they enter a single-page shopping platform that feels like a small shopping app rather than a form. They see product information and make ordinary shopping decisions. The system records both final choices and process data, such as whether users check reviews, how they respond to page changes, their click path, and their decision time. Short memory or arithmetic tasks separate the shopping rounds, so one decision has less influence on the next.

I will compare the scale answers with the interaction records. A short reflection question after the result page gives feedback about whether the comparison helped users notice a gap.

中文翻译：

这个文献缺口直接决定了我的作品设计。

用户先回答预算区间、商品兴趣和购物习惯等简短问题。然后，他们完成一个有五个选项、表述中性的自我认知量表。

接着，用户进入一个单页购物平台。它更像一个小型购物软件，而不是表单。用户会看到商品信息，并做出日常购物决定。系统记录最终选择和过程数据，例如用户是否查看评价、如何回应页面变化、点击路径和决策时间。每一轮购物之间有简短的记忆题或算术题，让前一轮决定对后一轮的影响更低。

我会把量表答案和互动记录进行比较。结果页后的简短反思问题，会反馈这种比较是否帮助用户注意到差距。

## Slide 6: Research Plan & Challenges

English:

At this stage, I have built a first website version, clarified the research focus, and collected the main sources.

The next task is to make the platform feel natural enough for users to become involved, while keeping the situations controlled enough for the records to be compared. I will then test the full process and revise the design from the interaction data.

The main challenges are methodological. The self-perception scale needs to measure real habits without leading users. Budget and price ranges need to feel believable across different product categories. I also need a clear way to connect answers, click paths, final choices, decision time, and reflection feedback in the final analysis.

Another design question is how to turn these relationship patterns into a consumer-profile label. The label should describe the pattern between self-perception and behavior, rather than judge the user.

中文翻译：

目前，我已经完成了第一版网站，明确了研究重点，也收集了主要文献。

下一步是让平台足够自然，使用户能够投入其中，同时让情境保持足够受控，方便比较记录。之后，我会测试完整流程，再根据互动数据修改设计。

主要挑战都在研究方法上。自我认知量表需要测到真实习惯，同时不能引导用户。预算和价格区间需要在不同商品类别中显得可信。我还需要在最终分析中找到清楚的方法，把答案、点击路径、最终选择、决策时间和反思反馈联系起来。

另一个设计问题是，怎样把这些关系模式转化成消费画像标签。标签应该描述自我认知和行为之间的模式，不评价用户本身。
