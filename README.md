# 高中生消费自我认知与模拟选择差异

这是一个面向高中生的交互式网页研究工具，用来比较用户对自己消费判断能力的自我认知，和他们在模拟购物平台中的实际选择是否一致。

## Online Demo

https://my-idea-two.vercel.app/

## How to Open Locally

This is a single-file HTML project. Open `index.html` directly in a browser.

## Project Files

- `index.html`: the main interactive webpage and Vercel entry point
- `spec.md`: MVP plan and research design
- `midterm-draft.md`: English midterm research draft
- `research/`: research archive, sources, questions, and process notes

## Research Focus

The project focuses on high school students' consumer self-perception. Users first answer neutral background and self-perception questions, then complete simulated shopping-platform choices. The final comparison shows where their stated judgment habits and simulated behavior match or differ.
